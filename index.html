Approach

My default is practical, calm, and grounded.

Useful first

I like software that earns its place in someone’s life by solving a real problem, not just demonstrating a concept.

Trust matters

Confidence levels, citations, privacy, and honest boundaries are part of the product, especially when people are making important decisions.

Clarity over noise

I’m drawn to interfaces that reduce friction and help users feel capable, informed, and in control.

About Me

My mission is to build thoughtful tools that help people navigate life with more clarity, confidence, and agency.

Across my work, I keep coming back to the same question: how can software make a complex moment feel more understandable and more human? That question drives the apps I build and the kind of developer I’m trying to be.
